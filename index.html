Pay + Daily

The Name

Pay — represents the financial and payment side of the system.
Daily — reflects the goal: real-time, everyday visibility into the business.

The Logo

The logo features a pencil, ruler, and books — representing the school supplies that the store is known for. The bold "P" stands for PayDaily and the Philippine Peso (₱), symbolizing the financial core of the system.
