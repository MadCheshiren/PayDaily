What PayDaily Does

Everything the Store Needs

Point-of-Sale

Staff can process transactions fast — select products, calculate totals, and print receipts in under 30 seconds.

Inventory Tracking

Stock levels update automatically after every sale. Get alerts before items run out — not after.

Sales Reports

Daily and monthly reports generated automatically. See best sellers, revenue trends, and product performance at a glance.

Role-Based Access

Managers get full access. Staff only see what they need. Sensitive data stays protected.

PayDaily

Pay + Daily

The Name

Pay — represents the financial and payment side of the system.
Teo — from PayTeodil Store, the business this system was built for.
Daily — reflects the goal: real-time, everyday visibility into the business.

The Logo

The logo features a pencil, ruler, and books — representing the school supplies that PayTeodil Store is known for. The bold "P" stands for both PayTeodil and the Philippine Peso (₱), symbolizing the financial core of the system.
